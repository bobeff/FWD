Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière

by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

This portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of three portraits of the Rivière family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 100x70 cm
Home: Musèe du Louvre, Paris
